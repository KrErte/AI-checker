# HireCheck Compliance — Outbound LinkedIn Templates

Use these for cold LinkedIn connection requests and InMails. **Rule of thumb:** personalise the first line with something specific from their profile (a recent post, a job opening, the ATS they mention in their stack). Never send more than 10 per day per account — LinkedIn will flag you.

**Tracking:** every link ends in `?ref=li-<persona>` so you can see which persona converts in Plausible.

---

## Mõõdikud (kuidas teada, kas töötab)

Räägi 10 inimesega esimesel nädalal ja mõõda:

| Mõõdik | Hea tulemus | Punane lipp |
|---|---|---|
| Response rate (InMail/connection) | ≥15% | <5% (muuda esimest lauset) |
| Call booked | ≥30% vastanutest | <10% (muuda pakkumist) |
| "Jah, maksaks 100€+/kuus" | ≥3/10 kõnest | 0–2/10 — mudel vale |
| Upfront raha / pilot pakkumine | ≥1 | 0 (pole veel valideeritud) |

Kui vähemalt 3 inimest 10-st ütleb "jah, maksaksin", **hakka ehitama MVP-d**. Kui 1–2, **muuda positsioneerimist**. Kui 0, **pööra sihtgruppi** (nt suurem ettevõte, teine persona).
