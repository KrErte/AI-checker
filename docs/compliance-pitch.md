## 🇬🇧 English

### The problem
From **2 August 2026**, every EU company that uses AI to screen, rank or filter job candidates is a **high-risk deployer** under Annex III, point 4 of the EU AI Act. As a deployer (Art. 26) you are legally required to:

- Maintain an inventory of every AI system in use
- Guarantee human oversight and document who reviews what
- Log every AI-assisted decision and retain it for 6 months
- Inform candidates and employee representatives
- Complete a **Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment** (Art. 27)
- Register in the EU database before use

**Fines reach €35M or 7% of global turnover.** Today, almost no HR team in Europe has any of this in place.

- **Decision audit trail** — every AI-assisted rejection logged
- **Candidate disclosure** templates in every EU language
- **Bias & GDPR Art. 22 monitoring**
