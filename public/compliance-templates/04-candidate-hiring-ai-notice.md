# Candidate Hiring AI Notice

**Version 1.0 · Required on your careers page if you use any AI in recruitment · Updates through 2 August 2026**

> Not legal advice. Starter text against EU AI Act Annex III §4 (hiring as HIGH-RISK from 2 August 2026), GDPR Art. 13, Art. 15 and Art. 22. This is the document HireCheck candidates are most likely to request from you.

## Why this matters more than any other template in the pack

Under the EU AI Act, "AI systems intended to be used for the recruitment or selection of natural persons" become high-risk from **2 August 2026**. Before that date, GDPR Art. 22 already applies whenever an automated decision "produces legal or similarly significant effects" on a candidate — which rejection from a job definitely is.

If you use an ATS keyword filter, an AI ranker, or any automated candidate scoring, you need this notice, and you need it on your careers page, not buried in your privacy policy. Every candidate you reject has the right to request it under Art. 15(1)(h) GDPR — and at hirecheck.eu we make that request easy.

## Where to publish

- Your careers / jobs page, above or next to the list of open roles.
- Linked from every job description.
- Linked from the confirmation email after a candidate applies.
- Linked from any rejection email.

*Last updated: {{YYYY-MM-DD}}*

We want you to understand how your application is handled from the moment you click "apply" to the moment you hear back from us.

### What AI we use

{{PICK THE OPTIONS THAT APPLY AND DELETE THE REST. IF NONE APPLY, REPLACE THIS ENTIRE SECTION WITH A CLEAR "WE DO NOT USE AI IN HIRING" STATEMENT.}}

- **Applicant tracking.** We use {{ATS_NAME}} to store and organise applications. This includes automated parsing of CVs into structured fields. No hiring decision is made on the basis of parsing alone.
- **Keyword matching.** Applications are automatically checked against required criteria in the job description (e.g. work authorisation, minimum years of experience). A recruiter manually reviews every match, including near-misses.
- **AI-assisted ranking.** Applications are scored by {{VENDOR_NAME}}'s model for relevance to the job description. A recruiter reviews all scores; they are advisory, not dispositive.
- **Automated rejection for knockout criteria only.** Candidates who do not meet legally required criteria (e.g. work authorisation) may receive an automated rejection. This is the only fully automated decision we make.

### How a human gets involved

Every application that clears the automated checks above is reviewed by a human recruiter before any hiring decision. No human-rated candidate is rejected without a human reading the application.

If you are rejected automatically, you will receive an email saying so. That email will include a link to this page and to our Article 22 request form.

### Your rights under the GDPR

Under European law, you have specific rights about how your application is handled:

- **Article 22(3)** — if you believe a decision about your application was made solely by automated processing and it had a significant effect on you (e.g. rejection), you have the right to obtain human intervention, to express your point of view, and to contest the decision.
- **Article 15(1)(h)** — you can request meaningful information about the logic involved in any automated processing, and the significance and envisaged consequences for you.
- **Article 13 and 14** — the information on this page is what these articles require us to tell you proactively.

### How to contest an automated decision

If your application was rejected and you believe the decision was made solely by automated processing:

1. Email **{{HIRING_EMAIL}}** with the subject line **"Article 22 Request — Human Review"**.
2. Include your full name, the date you applied, and the role you applied for.
3. We will acknowledge your request within 3 working days and reply with a human review decision within 14 working days. These are our internal commitments; the statutory maximum is one month under Art. 12(3) GDPR.

You can use the free generator at [hirecheck.eu/human-review](https://hirecheck.eu/human-review) to draft your request in English or Estonian. We accept requests from any EU language.
