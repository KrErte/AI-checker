### Your rights under EU law

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Article 15** — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you, plus meaningful information about any automated decision-making and the logic involved.
- **Article 22** — for any decision that produces a legal or similarly significant effect and is based *solely* on automated processing, you have the right to human intervention, to express your point of view, and to contest the decision.
- **Article 16 and 17** — rectification and erasure.
